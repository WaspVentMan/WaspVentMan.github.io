Help for understanding the Daily Air Quality Index (DAQI)

Hag's Delirium (HsD) is tied to the DAQI and the rain chance, the worse the day, the higher the phase. The value with a *star in front of the name is what is causing the HsD value. A phase of 1 or 2 means that nothing is wrong.

If it says "somewhere in [location]" it's because the API doesn't recognise your city/town.
